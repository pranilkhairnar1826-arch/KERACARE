⚡ About Keracare

Smart Health Records
For Every Worker

Keracare is Kerala's first digital health record system built specifically for migrant workers — ensuring no one loses their medical history when they move for a better life.

Who We Are

Healthcare that travels with you

Keracare is a secure, government-aligned digital health records platform designed for the 3.5 million migrant workers across Kerala. We believe your health history should never be left behind — whether you're working in Kochi, Bengaluru, or abroad.

With a simple smartphone and your unique QR Health ID, workers, doctors, and health centres can securely access your complete medical history anytime, anywhere.

🏥
⚠️

The Problem

Migrant workers frequently relocate for employment, carrying physical medical reports that are easily lost, damaged, or forgotten. When they fall ill in a new city, doctors have no access to their prior medical history.

  • Physical reports lost during migration
  • No continuity of care across states
  • Language barriers in new locations
  • Repeated expensive diagnostic tests
✅

Our Solution

Keracare gives every migrant worker a secure digital health vault — accessible via a unique QR Health ID. Doctors anywhere can instantly access verified health history with the worker's consent.

  • Secure cloud-based health record storage
  • QR Health ID for instant doctor access
  • Multilingual platform support
  • AI-powered health insights
What We Offer

Built for real-world healthcare needs

Every feature of Keracare is designed around one goal — making healthcare accessible and continuous for workers on the move.

📋
Digital Medical Records

Store prescriptions, lab reports, discharge summaries and vaccination records in one secure place — always accessible from your phone.

🪪
QR Health ID

Every worker gets a unique QR code that doctors can scan to instantly pull up their full medical history with patient consent.

📤
Easy Report Upload

Upload reports by simply photographing them. Our system auto-categorises and stores them securely in your health vault.

👨‍⚕️
Doctor Access to History

Treating doctors get a comprehensive, chronological view of the patient's health history — enabling faster, more accurate diagnosis.

🤖
AI Health Assistant

An intelligent assistant that answers health queries, reminds about medications, and flags unusual trends in your health data.

🔒
Privacy & Security

End-to-end encrypted records with consent-based access. Your data is always yours — shared only when you choose.
